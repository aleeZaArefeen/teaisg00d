body style

All body text should be rendered at a size of 10pts in the Verdana font (if possible),
in black and 14 points between lines. If the user does not have the
Verdana font installed, the list of fonts in the style sheet represents the order in which
the browser should search for fonts to use: Geneva, then Arial, then Helvetica. If the
user has none of those fonts, the browser will use whatever default sans-serif font is
available. Additionally, the page should have left, right, and top margins of 5 points.)

p.subheader

All text in the subheader class under the p element will be written in bold and colored
purple

img

All the images that are contained in the web page will have a padding of 3 points,
meaning that there will be a 3 point space around the image. The images on the web
page will also be moved to the right side of the page.

a

All text written under the a tag will have no text decoration applied to it and will be
displayed as a block, with a width of 30 as well as a height of 30.

a:link , a:visited

Applies to all links that have and have not been visited; appearing a baby blue colour

A:hover, a:active

Applies to links that have been clicked on or are being hovered over, turning them a
light orange colour

div.footer

Applies to where documentation of the website is placed, also known as a footer.
Text under the dev.footer tag will have a font size of 9 points, will be written in Italics
and will be placed in the center of the page. Text written here will also have a 30
points of space around it and a 12 point space underneath one another.
